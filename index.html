About Me

My name is Mr. Amit Chadha and I am a professional musician with more than 20 years of experience in the field of music. I started my professional journey in 2002 and since then I have been actively performing, teaching and working in the music industry. I hold a Master's degree in Music and have worked as a music teacher in well-known schools including Mayor World School, Innocent Hearts School, Eklavya School and British Olivia School. Along with teaching, I have been continuously performing in live shows and musical events. I have also worked internationally and performed in Bangkok on two different occasions. My goal is to continue spreading music through live performances, teaching and professional shows.
